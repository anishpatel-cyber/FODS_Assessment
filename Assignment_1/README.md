##### &#x20;Programs Included



Q2 – Arithmetic Operations

&#x20; Program that performs addition, multiplication, division, modulus, and exponentiation for two numbers.



Q3 – Number Type Checker

&#x20; Checks whether a number is positive or negative and also whether it is even or odd.



Q4 – Mathematical Calculations

&#x20; Calculates cube, cube root, logarithms, and power values of a given number.



Q5 – Marks Calculator

&#x20; Takes marks for six subjects and calculates total, average, percentage, and grade.



Q6 – Prime Numbers in Range

&#x20; Displays all prime numbers within a user-defined range and calculates their count and sum.



Q7 – Divisible Numbers Program

&#x20; Finds numbers that are divisible by 9 but not divisible by 6 within a given range.



Q8 – Factorial Program

&#x20; Calculates the factorial of a number and checks for invalid input.

&#x20; A simple game where the user tries to guess a randomly generated number.
